# Exercise Science Baseline

## Training Priorities
- Technique quality before load progression.
- Progressive overload via reps, load, sets, or density.
- Balance movement patterns: squat, hinge, push, pull, carry, core.

## Recovery
- Keep 1-2 reps in reserve for most sets.
- Sleep target: 7-9 hours.
- Deload or reduce volume if persistent fatigue appears.

## Injury Risk Control
- Warm-up 8-12 minutes with mobility + activation.
- Respect pain signals; modify range and exercise selection.
- For known injuries, prioritize stable and low-risk variants.
