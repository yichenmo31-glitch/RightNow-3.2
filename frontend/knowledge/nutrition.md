# Nutrition Baseline

## Core Principles
- Prioritize long-term adherence over extreme restriction.
- Protein target: 1.6-2.2 g/kg body weight/day.
- Fat target: 0.6-1.0 g/kg/day.
- Remaining calories from carbohydrates by activity demand.

## Energy Strategy
- Fat loss: TDEE - 300 to 500 kcal/day.
- Muscle gain: TDEE + 150 to 300 kcal/day.
- Recomposition: around maintenance with high protein.

## Meal Structure
- 3 main meals plus 0-1 snack if needed.
- Each main meal includes protein, vegetables/fruits, and quality carbs.
- Keep hydration and electrolyte intake consistent.

## Guardrails
- Avoid aggressive deficits when recovery is poor.
- Adjust by weekly trend, not single-day fluctuations.
