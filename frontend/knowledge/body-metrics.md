# Body Metrics and Formulas

## BMI
- BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)^2
- General range: 18.5-23.9 (regional criteria may vary).

## BMR (Mifflin-St Jeor)
- Male: 10*weight + 6.25*height - 5*age + 5
- Female: 10*weight + 6.25*height - 5*age - 161

## TDEE
- TDEE = BMR * activity multiplier
- Typical multipliers: 1.2, 1.375, 1.55, 1.725, 1.9

## Body Fat Estimate (screening only)
- Deurenberg estimate:
- Male: 1.2*BMI + 0.23*age - 16.2
- Female: 1.2*BMI + 0.23*age - 5.4
- Replace with measured values when available.
