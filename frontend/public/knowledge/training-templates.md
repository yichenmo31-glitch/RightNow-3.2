# Training Templates

## Beginner (3 days/week)
- Day 1: Full body A
- Day 2: Full body B
- Day 3: Full body A
- Session: 40-60 min, 5-7 exercises

## Intermediate (4 days/week)
- Day 1: Upper (push focus)
- Day 2: Lower
- Day 3: Upper (pull focus)
- Day 4: Lower + conditioning

## Advanced (5-6 days/week)
- Use split by goals: hypertrophy, strength, or mixed periodization.
- Include 1 low-fatigue day and 1 active recovery day weekly.

## Cardio Integration
- Fat loss phase: 2-4 sessions/week low to moderate intensity.
- Muscle gain phase: 1-2 sessions/week for conditioning support.
